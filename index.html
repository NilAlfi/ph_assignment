Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the practical implementation of ideas that result in the introduction of new goods services.

Sustainability

Sustainability is a social goal for people to co-exist on Earth over a long period of time.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the set of facilities and systems that serve a country, city, or other area

Empowerment

Empowerment is the degree of autonomy and self-determination in people and in communities.
